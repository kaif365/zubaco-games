1. OVERVIEW
========================================================================
Rapid Category Sort is a timed sorting game where words/items appear 
one at a time and the player must quickly swipe or tap left/right to 
sort each item into the correct category. Items auto-advance on a 
timer so speed is critical. Think Tinder-swipe meets word categorization.

Genre: Reflex / Classification / Time-pressure
Core loop: Item appears → Player swipes left or right → Correct/Wrong feedback → Next item

4. GAME MECHANICS
========================================================================

4.1 PHASES
- idle: Shows InstructionScreen, player clicks "Start"
- playing: Items appear one at a time, player swipes left/right
- finished: Shows ResultScreen with score breakdown

4.2 CATEGORY SYSTEM
- 16 category pairs in CATEGORY_BANK (Animal/Plant, Fruit/Vegetable, Hot/Cold, etc.)
- Each pair has 8 items per side (16 items total per pair)
- Game selects `categoryPoolSize` pairs from bank (shuffled by seed)
- Items interleaved from selected pairs then shuffled
- Category labels rotate: items shown in batches per pair

4.3 ITEM PRESENTATION
- Items appear one at a time with spring animation (FallingItem)
- Two half-screen tap zones (CategoryLanes): left (blue) and right (orange)
- Category labels shown at top-left/top-right
- Item auto-advances after itemIntervalMs (missed = penalty)
- feedbackColor: correct=emerald, wrong=red, default=gray

4.4 SCORING
- Correct: +pointsPerCorrect (default 10)
- Wrong: -penaltyPerWrong (default 5)
- Missed: -penaltyPerWrong (default 5)
- Final score = max(0, total)
- No time bonus — speed IS the score (faster = more items answered)

4.5 CONTROLS
- Touch: Tap left half or right half of screen
- Keyboard: ArrowLeft/a = left, ArrowRight/d = right

4.6 DEFAULT CONFIG (StageConfig)
- totalItems: 10
- itemIntervalMs: (varies by level)
- itemVisibleMs: (display time)
- timeLimitMs: 60000 (60s)
- pointsPerCorrect: 10
- penaltyPerWrong: 5
- categoryPoolSize: 3

6. AUDIO (8 sounds)
========================================================================
- itemSwipe: plays on player swipe
- sortCorrect: correct classification
- sortWrong: wrong classification
- combo: streak milestone reached
- levelComplete: game won
- gameOver: time expired
- timerWarning: timer low
- uiClick: menu interactions

========================================================================
7. BACKEND ENGINES
========================================================================
- achievementEngine: 10 achievements (First Win, Streak 5, Streak 10, Perfect, Speed Demon, etc.)
- boosterEngine: 5 boosters (Time Freeze, Hint, Skip, Double Points, Undo)
- comboEngine: streak tracking, multiplier step 0.25 per 3-streak, max 4.0×
- dailyChallenge: SHA-256 date-based seed, difficulty by day of week, 1.5× bonus
- leaderboard: standard
- scoreHistory: standard
- levelConfig: 10 levels (above table)

========================================================================
8. SAVE PROGRESS SYSTEM
========================================================================
- RapidSortProgressEntry: levelIndex, score, stars, completedAt, timeTakenMs, movesUsed, hintsUsed
- localStorage keys: 'rapid-sort-save-progress', 'rapid-sort-save-outbox'
- Functions: saveLevel, getProgress, getLevelProgress, getHighestCompletedLevel, getTotalScore
- Outbox pattern for offline sync
- Only saves if new score > existing score for that level
