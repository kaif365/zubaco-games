Spot The Difference 
 
Base Score Per Round 
100 pts 
Wrong Click Penalty 
−5 pts per wrong click 
Hint Penalty 
−5 pts per hint used 
Minimum Round Score 
0 pts 
Round Formula 
max(0, 100 − wrong_clicks×5 − hints×5) 
Time Bonus 
floor(10 × remaining time / total time) 
Final Score 
Sum of round scores + time bonus 
 
Sequence Recall 
Score Per Click 
total individual tile clicks that were correct across all 
successful rounds × scorePerClick (default: 10) 
Time Bonus 
floor(seconds left × bonusTimeRatio) — only if no wrong 
moves were made 
Default bonusTimeRatio 
1.0 
Final Score 
successful moves × scorePerClick + time bonus
