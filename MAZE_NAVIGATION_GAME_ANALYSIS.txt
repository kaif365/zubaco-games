───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 1. GAME CONCEPT
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Maze Navigation is a timed maze-running game. The player navigates a ball/beacon
through a grid-based maze (15×10 cells) using directional controls (UP/DOWN/
LEFT/RIGHT) to reach the goal. The game has multiple rounds per session, a 
countdown timer (120s), and scores based on completion speed and efficiency.

Core mechanic: Swipe or tap directional buttons to move through a maze. Walls
block movement. Reach the goal marker before time runs out. Multiple rounds per
game session with increasing difficulty.
