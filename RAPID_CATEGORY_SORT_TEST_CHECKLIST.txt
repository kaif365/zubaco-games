NOTES
========================================================================
- Tinder-swipe style: items appear one at a time, swipe left/right
- 16 category pairs (Animal/Plant, Fruit/Vegetable, Hot/Cold, etc.)
- 8 items per side per pair (16 items total per pair)
- Auto-advance: unanswered items count as missed (penalty)
- No time bonus — speed IS the score (faster response = fewer misses)
- Default: 10 items, 60s timer, +10/correct, -5/wrong, -5/missed, 3 categories
- Level progression: time 60s→30s, items 10→30, categories 3→6
- Controls: touch (half-screen taps) + keyboard (arrows/a/d)
- Audio: 8 sounds (itemSwipe, sortCorrect, sortWrong, combo, etc.)
- Backend: 5 boosters, combo engine (4× max, step 0.25 per 3-streak), daily challenge
- State: Custom hooks (useState + useCallback + useRef), @tanstack/react-query for client
- BrowserRouter with single catch-all route → GamePage handles everything
- Confetti triggers on 8+ correct answers
