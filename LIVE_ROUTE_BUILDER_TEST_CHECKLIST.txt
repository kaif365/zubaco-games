 Star Rating System:
   ★★★ (3 stars) — Path efficiency ≥ 90% or score ≥ nodeCount × 15
   ★★☆ (2 stars) — Path efficiency ≥ 70% or score ≥ nodeCount × 10
   ★☆☆ (1 star)  — Level completed (any score)

 Achievement Triggers:
   FIRST_WIN         — Complete 1 level
   STREAK_3          — 3-combo streak
   STREAK_5          — 5-combo streak
   PERFECT_ROUTE     — 3 perfect games
   SPEED_DEMON       — 3 fast completions
   LEVEL_5           — Reach level 5
   LEVEL_10          — Reach level 10
   SCORE_1000        — Score 1000+ in single game
   DEDICATED         — Play 50+ games
   NO_MISTAKES       — (reserved for future)
