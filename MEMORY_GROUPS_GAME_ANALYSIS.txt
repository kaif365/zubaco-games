3. GAME MECHANICS
========================================================================
Phases:
- idle → Waiting to start, shows instructions
- memorize → Words displayed face-up for memorization (5s default)
- play → Timer counts down, player groups words by category
- ended → Game over, shows score & submits to API

Core Gameplay:
- 9 words displayed in 3×3 grid (3 groups × 3 words each)
- Words belong to semantic categories (Fruits, Colors, Planets, etc.)
- Player memorizes word groupings during memorize phase
- After memorize, words shuffle; player selects 3 at a time
- "Lock Group" button submits a group of 3 selected words
- 3 groups total to submit within time limit (60s default)
- Scoring: points per correct group + partial credit + time bonus

Word Sets (8 predefined):
- Fruits/Colors/Planets, Animals/Tools/Sports, Instruments/Countries/Elements
- Vehicles/Foods/Weather, Clothes/Trees/Dances, Gems/Seas/Metals
- Birds/Flowers/Languages, Drinks/Shapes/Emotions

Scoring:
- pointsPerGroup: 50 (full correct group)
- pointsPerPartialWord: 10 (word in wrong group but correct category)
- timeBonusMultiplier: 3 (seconds_remaining × 3)

Config:
- showDurationMs: 5000 (memorize time)
- timeLimitMs: 60000 (play time)
- groupSize: 3
- totalGroups: 3
