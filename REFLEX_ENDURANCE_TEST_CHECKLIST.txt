NOTES
========================================================================
- Whack-a-mole style: circles spawn, tap ONLY green
- 4 colors: green (40%), red/blue/yellow (20% each)
- Endurance: survive 5 minutes, speed accelerates
- Death: 2 wrong taps max (instant game over on 3rd)
- Circles auto-disappear after 2 seconds (no penalty for missed green)
- Speed: initialInterval × 0.85^(elapsed/30000), min 300ms
- Default: 5min timer, 1200ms initial spawn, 2 max wrong
- Level progression: time 60s→35s, rounds 10→30, reaction 1000ms→500ms
- Audio: 7 sounds (targetAppear, targetHit, targetMiss, combo, etc.)
- Backend: 5 boosters, combo engine, daily challenge, timingAnalyzer
- State: Custom hooks (useState + useCallback + useRef), NO Zustand
- No React Router — App.tsx renders GamePage directly
- Confetti triggers on score >= 20
